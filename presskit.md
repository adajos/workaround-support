## One-Liner

Wordaround is a free word puzzle game where two letters are locked in place and you build as many words as you can around them.

---

## Description

Most word games give you total freedom over letter placement. Wordaround does the opposite: every puzzle starts with two **locked letters** fixed in random positions. Your job is to fill in the rest to form valid words — as many as you can find.

It's a simple constraint that creates a surprisingly deep challenge. The locked letters force you to think differently about word construction, rewarding both vocabulary and lateral thinking. Some configurations are wide open with dozens of possibilities. Others leave you staring at two awkward consonants wondering if any five-letter word in the English language has a K in the second slot and a Z in the fourth.

Wordaround features three distinct game modes:

**Puzzle of the Day** — A new puzzle every day, the same for everyone worldwide. Difficulty builds through the week: Monday starts with five-letter words, and by Friday you're tackling seven-letter puzzles. Share your score without spoiling the answer.

**Practice Mode** — Unlimited puzzles at any difficulty. Choose your word length and play at your own pace — great for warming up or winding down.

**Progressive Mode** — The locked letters stay the same, but their positions shift after each round. Difficulty ramps up naturally as the easy configurations give way to trickier ones. No timer, no pressure — just you and the letters.

The game uses a comprehensive English word list with thousands of valid words, including plurals, verb forms, and plenty of words that will send you to the dictionary. Points scale with word length and letter rarity.

---

## What Makes It Different

**The locked letter mechanic** is the core of Wordaround and what sets it apart from other word games. Rather than unscrambling letters or filling a grid, you're working within a constraint — two letters you can't move — and finding every word you can build around them. It's a small twist that changes how you think about words entirely.

**Vibes** let you customize the look and feel of the game. Each Vibe is a combination of a color theme and background music that changes the entire atmosphere. Play in Focus mode when you want to concentrate, switch to Chill for a relaxed session, or try Energy for something more upbeat. It's a small feature that makes the game feel personal — and seasonal and special Vibes are planned for future updates.

**Completely free, no catches.** There are no ads, no gated features, and no timers blocking you from playing. Every mode, every difficulty, every Vibe is available from the first launch. The only in-app purchases are optional tip jars for players who want to support an indie developer — they don't unlock anything.

## About the Developer

Wordaround is built by Josh, a professional iOS engineer by day and indie game developer by night. With years of experience building apps at a major software company, Josh built Wordaround as a personal project — a chance to have complete creative control over something from concept to launch.

Every line of code, every design decision, and every sound effect in Wordaround was made by one person. It's the kind of app that can only exist when a developer has the freedom to obsess over details: the subtle haptic feedback when you place a letter, the way found words slide into the list, the background music that knows its place.
