# Wordaround Support

**Wordaround** is a word puzzle game where two anchor letters are locked in place and you build as many words as you can around them. Available on iPhone and iPad.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is the daily puzzle scored?**
Points are based on word length and letter rarity. Longer words and words using uncommon letters score higher.

**Is an internet connection required?**
No. All puzzles are generated locally on your device. You can play entirely offline.

**Does the daily puzzle change based on my time zone?**
The daily puzzle is the same for everyone worldwide on a given calendar date, based on your device's local date.

**What word list does Wordaround use?**
Wordaround uses a comprehensive English word list that includes common words and their standard forms (plurals, past tense, etc.). Some words may be unfamiliar â€” that's part of the challenge!

**Can I sync my progress across devices?**
Not currently, but this is something I'm considering for a future update.

## Privacy Policy

Wordaround does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. All game data is stored locally on your device.

The app does not require an account or sign-in of any kind.

**In short: your data stays on your device. That's it.**
